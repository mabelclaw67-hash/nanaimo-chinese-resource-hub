Overview

服务概览

The Nanaimo Chinese Resource Hub is intended to share information, improve access to local support, and provide practical tools that are easy to use and update.

纳奈莫华人资源平台旨在分享信息、提升本地服务获取效率，并提供便于使用和更新的实用工具。

This website may include community contacts, provider listings, and editable templates for communication and organization.

本网站可能包含社区联系信息、服务提供者列表，以及用于沟通和整理事务的可编辑模板。
